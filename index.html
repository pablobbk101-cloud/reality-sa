Key Issues

What's Happening

⚡

Load Shedding

Up to 12hrs/day outages

Eskom's failing grid has plunged South Africa into rolling blackouts for over a decade, crippling businesses and households alike.

→ 📉

Unemployment

~32% official rate

One of the highest unemployment rates in the world. Youth unemployment exceeds 60%, leaving an entire generation without prospects.

→ 🔒

Crime & Safety

High murder & robbery rates

South Africa consistently ranks among the most dangerous countries globally, with violent crime affecting communities across all provinces.

→ 🏛️

Corruption

Billions lost annually

State capture, tender fraud, and institutional corruption have gutted public services and undermined trust in government at every level.

→ 💸

Cost of Living

Inflation squeezing households

Food, fuel and electricity prices continue to rise while wages stagnate, pushing millions deeper into poverty each year.

→ 💧

Water Shortages

Failing municipal infrastructure

Ageing pipes, municipal mismanagement and drought are leaving communities without reliable clean water — a basic human right.
