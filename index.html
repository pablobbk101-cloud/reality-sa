How Mr Point scores devices

Every review uses the same 5-factor system — scored out of 10, rolled into one overall Mr Point Score.

⚡
Performance
How fast it opens apps, handles multitasking, gaming, and whether it stutters under real-world use.
🖥️
Display
Screen quality, resolution, brightness outdoors, refresh rate, and how it feels to actually use.
🔋
Battery / Thermals
How long it lasts on a charge and whether it gets dangerously hot under load.
🔨
Build Quality
Materials, sturdiness, keyboard feel (on laptops), and whether it feels like it's worth what you paid.
💰
Value for Money
Whether what you're paying matches what you're getting. Always the most important score.
